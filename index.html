Transport Options

If possible, we highly recommend choosing a flight with SAW as the destination airport, as it would be much easier for local transportation.

From IST Airport to Campus

There are 2 choices.

1. Taxi: The easiest way to get to the campus is to take a taxi from the airport. The cost is around 50 euros, and it takes around ~60 minutes to get to the campus.

2. Another option is to use HAVAIST shuttle from IST to Sabiha Gökçen Airport (SAW) and then take a taxi to the campus from there. However, the HAVAIST schedules are limited, so you might need to wait at the airport, but it will be much cheaper.

Please check HERE for more information on HAVAIST schedule and the shuttle line, and choose "ISTANBUL HAVALIMANI" to "SABIHA GOKCEN AIRPORT", and hit "List Trips".

From SAW Airport to Campus

Taxi: The easiest way to get to the campus is to take a taxi from the airport. The cost is around 10 euros, and it takes around ~15 minutes to get to the campus.
